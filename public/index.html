Twitter Resources for COVID emergencies

This platform is majorly a bot on Twitter. You can tag @ResourcesBot under an SOS tweet and for it, the bot fetches latest verfied tweets. There is room for error and such, and for that, you can have another look on this website. This also helps if you're not on Twitter. This app is in prototype stage, it was released early considering how dire the situation is. There are a bunch of features left to implement. Since we have not added a bunch of core features, the data might take 5-10 seconds to load. Feel free to reach out to us via Twitter for errors or corrections.
